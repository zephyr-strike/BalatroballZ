# BalatroballZ
A kinda unbalanced Dragonball content mod for Balatro.

Current additions:
- 20 Common Jokers
- 22 Uncommon Jokers
- 12 Rare Jokers
- 5 Legendary Jokers
- 9 Jokers that can transform, with most having multiple stages (base forms are vanilla rarities)
- 4 Fusion Jokers, 3 of which have transformations (that don't actually fuse)
- 4 new enhancements
- A new consumable type called Training Grounds which can add editions and new enhancements to your playing cards, complete with Capsule Boosters from which to obtain them

Art sourced from Dragon Ball Legends, Dragon Ball: The Breakers, Dragon Ball Super Card Game Fusion World and @blz151101 on Twitter/X
